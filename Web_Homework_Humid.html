Summary: Lattitude vs. Humidity

Humidity is directly related to temperature and regional availability of water.The rate of evaporation at a given place is always dependent on the humidity of that place because if the air is already filled with water vapor, it will not have any place to hold excess vapor and therefore, evaporation will occur at an extremely slow rate.Now consider if both the hot and cold areas have the same relative humidity of 70 percent. In this case, when the two wet cloths are spread in these two different temperature regions, what will be the rate of evaporation? It will still be more in the hot region as hot air has more capacity to hold water vapor than the cold air.
