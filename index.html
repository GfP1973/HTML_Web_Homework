Meteorological Analysis: Primary Influences on Weather

Previous Next

Some of the world's greatest population centers are located within 100 miles of the equator. Equatorial weather, therefore has significant influence over the world's various cultures and economies, and plays a role in shaping our interactions globally. Understanding the weather patterns and impacts can provide insights into global interactions. This study used open source weather data to examine weather patterns observed at 500 equatorial cities focusing on temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and wind velocity.
